Senior housing is quietly the biggest housing story of 2026. REITs Welltower (WELL -1.46%), Ventas (VTR -3.00%), and Healthpeak (DOC -3.68%) have invested billions into senior housing. Occupancy hit a record 89.1% in 2025 and could cross 90% this year. Why it matters: Boomers turning 80 means more downsizing inventory coming to market, and buyers competing for senior-living units will bump against institutional capital. For our Fremont and Tri-Valley agents, this is the listing conversation with parents-in-law and longtime owners who have been sitting on equity.
For listing agents: Fresh supply cooled today. If you have a listing with strong pending interest, don't be shy about tightening review dates. Buyer urgency is real when inventory dips, even on a one-day sample.
Retail is also returning. Not a Bay Area headline but a signal that commercial landlords are finally finding bottom.
