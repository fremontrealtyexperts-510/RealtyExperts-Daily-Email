February Market: Buyers Gaining Leverage

The typical home that went under contract in February spent 66 days on the market — the slowest February pace in a decade. The typical buyer scored 1.8% off the list price — the biggest February discount since 2023; sellers outnumber buyers, giving buyers negotiating power.

Pending home sales and new listings both inched down last month, while home prices inched up. The Bay Area market is "quietly accelerating" into the spring season; while overall inventory remains tight, single-family homes in Alameda County have climbed back to a median of $1.3 million, showing resilience despite higher rates.
